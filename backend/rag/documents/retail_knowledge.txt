Smart Retail Assistant Knowledge Base

Retail analytics helps businesses monitor sales, inventory, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. A smart retail system combines machine learning, forecasting, recommendation systems, and AI-powered assistants to improve decision-making.

Key Retail Metrics:

* Revenue: Total money earned from product sales.
* Profit: Revenue minus operational and product costs.
* Inventory Level: Number of items currently available in stock.
* Average Order Value (AOV): Revenue divided by total orders.
* Customer Retention Rate: Percentage of returning customers.
* Forecasted Sales: Predicted future sales using machine learning models.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) in Retail:
RAG systems retrieve relevant business documents and combine them with LLM responses to provide accurate answers. Retail chatbots use RAG for answering FAQs, product queries, inventory details, and sales insights.

Vector Databases:
Vector databases store embeddings generated from retail documents. FAISS is commonly used for semantic search in RAG systems.

Embeddings:
Embeddings convert text into numerical vectors for similarity search. SentenceTransformers and HuggingFace embedding models are commonly used.
